pricing FAQs

Here are some of the FAQs we have been asked about our Pricings

Do I have to enter my Credit or Master Card to sign up?

No. We only need you to fill out the form, then you will be able to start building your first chatbot for free. We will never ask an access to your either Credit or Master card.

When is exactly my free trial period is over?

We will give you full access for our chat builder regardless of your pricing choice for 10 days, after we revoke your privilege unless you subscribed for one of our pricing options.

Can I cancel my subscription when ever I want?

Yes, anytime. If you want to cancel your subscription just contact as and we will cancel your subscription instantly.

What happens if I got questions to answer but I reached the maximum responses of my plan?

We will not provide you an extra response privilege! Your Chatbots will continue to respond to users included in your plan but will no longer respond to users outside your plan. If such things keep happening monthly, we recommend you to upgrade to a higher plan.
